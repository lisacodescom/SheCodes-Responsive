Yogurt App

Yogurt is a dairy product made by fermenting milk with a bacterial yogurt culture. It provides protein and calcium, and it may enhance healthy probiotic gut bacteria. Health benefits range from protecting against osteoporosis to relieving irritable bowel disease and aiding digestion1. Yogurt can be a tasty, nutritious addition to any diet. However, there are plenty of different yogurts, and some are more healthful than others1.
